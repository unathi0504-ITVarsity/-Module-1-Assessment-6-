Hugo Boss is a German fashion and modus vivendi house, established in 1924 by Hugo Boss. Originating in Metzingen and still based there today, the company offers multiple lines of fashions and accessories under the core brands Hugo and Boss. The Boss brand offers the subsequent lines: Boss Black, modern classic clothing and the broadest range of products; Boss Orange, more unique and Bohemian in style; Boss Selection, high-end menswear; Boss green, focusing on sports and active wear. The Hugo brand is renown for its fashion-forward, European-influenced styles. The company produces fragrances in conjunction with Proctor & Gamble prestige. Fragrances for men and ladies are offered underneath both the Boss and Hugo brands. The first fragrance was Boss number one for men, launched in 1985. The earliest edition was created in 1985 and the newest is from 2019. Hugo Boss fragrances were made in collaboration with perfumers Jean-Marc Chaillan, Pierre Wargnye, Ursula Wandel, Will Andrews, Sophie Labbe, Alain Astori, Beatrice Piquet, Annick Menardo, Calice Becker, Domitille Michalon, Ilias Ermenidis, Gerard Anthony, Bruno Jovanovic, Pascal Gaurin, Louise Turner, Nathalie Feisthauer, Bob Aliano and Claude Dir.
